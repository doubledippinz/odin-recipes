Burger Recipe

Description

Who doesn't love a juicy burger? You'll never have another burger after this one..

  • Buns
  • Beef Mince
  • Cheese Slices
  • Mouldy Ketchup
  1. Tickle the mince and then spit on it
  2. Slap a bap on each side of the juicy mince
  3. Sneak the cheese in somewhere, let it be a surprise
  4. Mouldy ketchup on the top of the bun
  5. Leave outside in hot sun for 2 days
  6. Enjoy!
